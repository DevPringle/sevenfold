Sevenfold Shuts Down Operations

Published
December 15, 2024
Sevenfold Team

After serving the World of Tanks competitive community, Sevenfold has officially ceased all operations. Our matchmaking services, leaderboards, and tournament systems are no longer available.

All game queues, statistical tracking, Discord integration, and competitive features have been permanently shut down. Player data has been securely archived and is no longer accessible through any platform.

To our dedicated community - thank you for being part of Sevenfold. The competitive matches, friendships formed, and memorable moments made this journey worthwhile. Your passion for World of Tanks and competitive gaming drove everything we built.

While we had plans to expand into a full web platform, we've made the decision to wind down operations instead. We're grateful for the time we had together and the community we built.

Thank you for playing with us.

- The Sevenfold Team
